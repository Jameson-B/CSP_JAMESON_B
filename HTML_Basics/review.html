Computer Science Principles is an introductory course to the field of computer science in general, covering topics from hardware to programming to the Internet.

Basics

What is a computer?
A computer is anything that stores and manipulates information.
Hardware and Software
Hardware is the physical components of the computer.
Software is the instructions that the computer runs on.
Binary
A base-2 number system.
Made of 0 and 1.
One place represents a power of 2.
Ex: 0101 = 5
