HTML Advanced Notes

A nested tag is a tag the exists inside of another tag. Some tags require nesting while others do not.

An attribute is further instructions for the computer given inside the opening tag, such as the id tag or the charset tag.

Lists are just like the lists you make in real life, and they require li tags inside of other tags.
